MedPass

MedPass is a WebApp that stores on IPFS a small file with medical relevant information for emergencies and makes it available on the fly with a QR code. Helping your physician make faster and better decision. The QR code can be embedded on a bracelet, necklace, tattoo, or even on an inplated on an NFC. A tag will specify that it should be scan in case of an emergency. So scanning the code will give access to the information.
